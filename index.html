About Me

My name is Purushothaman, and I am currently a Junior Research Fellow at the Materials Modeling and Simulation Laboratory at the Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing, Chennai since March 2023. I am writing to express my interest in the Research Assistant position in the theoretical design of materials for metal-ion batteries at your esteemed Inverse Materials Design group.

In my current role, I am engaged in the project titled "Defect and Strain Engineered Correlated Oxides as Cost-effective and High-performance Transparent Conductors," funded by DST-SERB, Government of India. This project has enabled me to develop a strong expertise in computational materials science, particularly using Density Functional Theory (DFT) to understand and optimize material properties.

Research

My experience includes working with DFT functionals (including GGA-PBE) and the DFT+U method within the Vienna Ab initio Simulation Package (VASP) to accurately compute electronic structures. My focus has been on optimizing optical and transport properties in strongly correlated oxides for transparent conducting applications to replace traditional Indium Tin Oxide (ITO). For instance, I utilized DFT to investigate the impact of strain on the optoelectronic properties of SrVO3 which is a gapped metal and explored the effects of doping early 4d and 5d transition metals on the V-site, which resulted in a significant shift in the optical absorption spectra suitable for transparent conducting applications. These findings are currently being compiled into a manuscript.

Beyond DFT calculations, I am an intermediate user of Python programming for post-processing and visualizing VASP Outputs, utilizing modules such as VASPKIT and BoltzTraP for in-depth material property analysis. Additionally, I have contributed to projects beyond my primary focus, such as assisting a senior colleague in analyzing d-band centers for CO2 reduction reactions, demonstrating my ability to work collaboratively in a research environment.
